About

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch painter who is one of the most famous and influential figures in the history of art. In just over a decade, he created about 2,100 art pieces. This includes around 860 oil paintings, most of which date from the last two years of his life. They include landscapes, portraits and self-portraits, and are characterised by bold colours and expressive brush strokes. All these factors contributed to the foundations of modern art.

Gallery

Starry Night 1889

This painting is Starry Night by Van Gogh. This is one of his most famous pieces, which was painted in 1889. One of the most beautiful paintings he painted. Fun fact; Vincent gained a lot of his fame due to his brother widow, Johanna van Gogh-Bonger, spreading and showing his work after his death.

Vase with Fifteen Sunflowers

Another one of Vincent van Gogh's most famous paintings. He painted it in 1888 during his time in Arles, France. Even though this painting was not set at night, many of his paintings were inspired by the night because he was fascinated by it.

Van Gogh Self-portrait

Van Gogh's Self-portrait is among the most famous and expressive works he made in France in 1889. It was painted during his stay at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum in France. Did you know he had his own style which featuring thick, expressive strokes, made his work instantly recognizable and highly influential to all art.

Wheatfield with Crows

Vincent van Gogh's Wheatfield with Crows is one of his final paintings and was painted in 1890. It is interpreted it as him expressing his inner conflicts. Many impressions were only made after his death because he actually did not have any fame until he passed away.

The Potato Eaters

The Potato Eaters is one of Van Gogh's less popular paintings and was painted in 1885. It was painted to try and capture rural life and the life of peasants. Another fun fact is that Vincent actually did not start painting until he was 27 unlike many artists who started when they were young.

Café Terrace at Night

Vincent van Gogh's painting Café Terrace at Night is popular but not famous compared to his other paintings. It was painted in 1888 and was meant to capture the warmth of the Cafe as well as the gloomy atmosphere of night. Did you know that due to lack of Canvas's Vincent resorted to painting on top of old paintings he made.
